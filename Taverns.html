Greek Traditional Cuisine

Laterna (Greek – Traditional cuisine)

Laterna is a Greek tavern celebrating the tradition of sharing good food and good company. We serve authentic Greek meze made with seasonal, locally sourced ingredients from classic dips and cheeses to grilled seafood and regional specialties. With a warm, welcoming atmosphere and a curated selection of wines, ouzo, and tsipouro, Laterna offers a true taste of Greek hospitality and a comforting, soulful dining experience.

TripAdvisor | Google Maps

Kardamo

The Kardamo restaurant in Kalamata is one of the most recognizable dining spots in the city. At 21 Sidirodromikou Stathmou Street, you will find a youthful and modern space adorned with wood and stone that will impress you, while the restaurant's jazz music selections will take you on a journey.

TripAdvisor | Google Maps

To Xagiati

At the Kalamata marina, you’ll find one of the city’s most timeless spots for culinary enjoyment: Chagiati. With a history spanning over 30 years, this traditional tavern is a staple for locals as well as visitors seeking authentic flavors and hospitality with character.

TripAdvisor | Google Maps

Leonidas Butcher Tavern

The dishes, ranging from lamb chops to sausages and offal, are prepared with care and expertise. The warm, welcoming atmosphere of the tavern combines with authentic Greek hospitality, making it ideal for family and friendly gatherings. The restaurant’s commitment to customer satisfaction is matched by consistently high-quality options in the realm of meat dishes.

Fish Taverns

Ksimeroma Kourkoutas

One of Kalamata’s famous seafood taverns, Ximeroma, is located on Vergas Beach and is the perfect choice if you want to enjoy delicious, authentic flavors. The restaurant recommends dishes such as an exceptional shrimp pasta that will have you licking your fingers for sure. With an unparalleled view of the beautiful Kalamata bay, it offers a combination that will leave no one indifferent.

TripAdvisor | Google Maps

To Limeni

It is renowned for its flavorful local specialties with fish and the best traditional Greek cuisine. Dine in our shady garden in a very quiet and pleasant setting, just a few steps from the sea. On cooler evenings, the spacious indoor dining area offers the perfect atmosphere for a warm and family-friendly experience.

Almyra

Visitors enjoy relaxation and fun with views of the Messinian Bay and the city of Kalamata. Known for its quality cuisine, signature cocktails, and music inspired by international, Cuban, and funk sounds, Almyra offers a welcoming, seaside atmosphere under the natural shade of tamarisk trees. With sunbeds, ping-pong, and options for swimming, coffee, or a special dining experience, it’s an ideal spot by the sea.

TripAdvisor | Google Maps

Secada

Secada Restaurant is a hidden gem that deserves more recognition for its exceptional dining experience. Nestled in a beautifully airy, two-level space, it captures the essence of modern Central America while offering a relaxed atmosphere. The menu features an impressive array of dishes, from flavorful sea bass to tender pork tenderloin, all artfully presented and bursting with taste.

TripAdvisor | Google Maps

Ginger

GinGer is a charming cocktail bar located in the beautiful coastal town of Kalamata, Greece. With its stunning seaside views and inviting atmosphere, it stands out as a must-visit destination for tourists seeking a blend of relaxation and vibrant social experiences. The bar features a diverse menu of expertly crafted cocktails, each made with fresh, local ingredients, ensuring a unique taste that reflects the essence of the region.
